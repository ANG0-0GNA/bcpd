Supplementary Video 1 -- A Bayesian Formulation of Coherent Point Drift

This is a demonstration of a point matching algorithm, Bayesian coherent point drift. The detail of the algorithm is available in the following paper: Osamu Hirose, "A Bayesian formulation of coherent point drift", IEEE TPAMI, 2020.
